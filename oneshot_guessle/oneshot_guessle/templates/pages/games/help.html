How to play {{request.site.name}}?
  1. You have one chance or one shot to guess the random word or Guessle of the day
  2. There are 5 clues to help you guess the day's random guessle
  3. Type the word and press enter to submit the guess
  4. Each of the 5 clues have their letters coloured to help you guess the Guessle of the day
  5. Orange indicates the letter is present in target word but not in the same location
  6. Green indicates the letter is present in target word in the same location
  7. Gray indicates letter is not present in target word
  8. There are 3 stars to collect every day. There is the main game, the easy edition and a special harder game for members who are supporters on Ko-Fi
  9. The streak is currently only based on the main game and not the easy and hard daily Guessles
